Common Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name, then Subscriptions. Find Scripted and tap Cancel Subscription.

How do I delete my account?

Go to the More tab inside the app and select Delete Account. Your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

My script didn't generate. What do I do?

Check your internet connection and try again. If the issue persists, email us and we'll look into it.

I was charged but don't have access.

Try restoring your purchase inside the app, or contact us at tryscripted@gmail.com with your Apple ID email.

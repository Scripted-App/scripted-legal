Artificial Intelligence and Data Processing

Scripted uses artificial intelligence to create personalized scripts. Here is how your data is processed:

Script Generation

  • Your responses to guided questions are sent to our AI service provider (Anthropic's Claude API) to generate personalized scripts
  • These responses are processed in real-time and are not stored by the AI provider for model training purposes
  • Your personal information is not used to train any machine learning models

Text-to-Speech Conversion

  • Your generated script text is sent to Google Cloud Text-to-Speech to create audio
  • Audio is generated on-demand and stored in your account
  • Google processes the text solely for audio generation and does not use your content for other purposes

Our Commitment

  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do not use your content to train AI models
  • Your scripts and responses remain private to your account

Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data Is Stored

  • Account information and scripts are stored using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform)
  • Audio files are stored in Firebase Cloud Storage
  • All data is stored on secure, industry-standard cloud infrastructure

Security Measures

  • All data transmission is encrypted using TLS/SSL
  • User authentication is handled through Firebase Authentication
  • Database access is restricted through security rules
  • Audio files are only accessible to authenticated account owners
  • We implement access logging and monitoring

Data Retention

  • Your data is retained as long as your account is active
  • You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time
  • Upon account deletion, your personal data and scripts will be permanently removed within 30 days
  • Anonymized usage statistics may be retained for analytical purposes
